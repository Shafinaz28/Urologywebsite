When should I consult a urologist?

+

If you are experiencing urinary symptoms, kidney stone pain, prostate concerns, infertility issues or recurring infections, consultation is recommended.

Do all urology problems need surgery?

+

Not always. Many conditions can be managed through careful evaluation, medication and minimally invasive options where appropriate.

Do you provide treatment for kidney stones and prostate issues?

+

Yes. The clinic covers a broad range of kidney, urinary, prostate and uro-surgical concerns with a tailored treatment approach.
