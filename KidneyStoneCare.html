Kidney Stone Care

We provide advanced diagnosis and effective treatment for kidney stones using modern, minimally invasive techniques. Our goal is to help patients get fast relief from pain, prevent complications, and ensure long-term kidney health.

Expert Care for Kidney Stones

Kidney stones can cause severe pain, discomfort, and urinary problems if left untreated. Our specialized urology team offers complete evaluation and personalized treatment plans based on the size, type, and location of the stone.

From medication and lifestyle guidance to advanced laser and endoscopic procedures, we ensure safe, precise, and patient-friendly care for faster recovery.

  • ✔ Comprehensive kidney stone evaluation
  • ✔ Minimally invasive treatment options
  • ✔ Advanced endoscopic and laser procedures
  • ✔ Personalized recovery and prevention guidance

Treatment Options We Offer

URSL

Ureteroscopic Lithotripsy (URSL) is a minimally invasive procedure used to remove or break stones located in the ureter using a small endoscope and laser technology.

PCNL

Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL) is recommended for larger kidney stones. It involves removing stones through a small incision in the back with high precision.

RIRS

Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery (RIRS) is an advanced laser procedure used to treat stones inside the kidney without any external cuts, ensuring quicker recovery and less discomfort.

Common Symptoms

  • • Severe pain in the back, side, or lower abdomen
  • • Burning sensation while urinating
  • • Blood in urine
  • • Frequent urge to urinate
  • • Nausea and vomiting
  • • Fever in case of infection
