Our Services

Female Urology

We provide dedicated female urology care for urinary leakage, bladder control issues, pelvic support concerns and recurring urinary problems with modern, patient-friendly treatment options.

Personalized Care for Women’s Urinary Health

Female urology focuses on urinary and pelvic floor concerns that can affect comfort, confidence and daily life. Symptoms such as urine leakage, urgency, frequent urination and bladder discomfort should not be ignored.

Our clinic offers careful evaluation and individualized treatment plans designed to improve bladder control, reduce symptoms and support better long-term quality of life.

  • ✔ Dedicated care for female urinary issues
  • ✔ Advanced treatment for incontinence and bladder symptoms
  • ✔ Minimally invasive support options
  • ✔ Personalized diagnosis and follow-up care

Treatment Options We Offer

Stress Urinary Incontinence

Evaluation and treatment for urine leakage during coughing, sneezing, laughing or physical activity to improve daily comfort and confidence.

TOT / TVT Repair

TOT and TVT are supportive sling procedures used to treat selected cases of urinary incontinence and improve bladder control.

Overactive Bladder Care

Structured treatment for urgency, frequent urination and sudden bladder symptoms to help patients regain better bladder management.

Common Concerns We Treat

  • • Urinary leakage during activity
  • • Sudden urge to urinate
  • • Frequent urination during day or night
  • • Bladder discomfort or incomplete control
  • • Prolapse-related urinary symptoms
  • • Recurrent urinary infections and irritation
